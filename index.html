The rules

Rules are to be followed by all members of the chat.

Be respectful to everyone in the STT Tribe! We are all here for the love of our videogames!

  • Keep chat in the appropriate room to avoid flooding other chats with unnecessary materials. For example, post news articles in the news chat.
  • Any racism, racial slurs, homophobic slurs or other discrimination will not be tolerated.
  • Do not post phone numbers or e-mail addresses in the main chat. We strongly encourage you not to share any personal information at all, but if you choose so please do it via private message at your own risk. (Not everyone is as trustworthy as they may seem.)
  • Stay Humble!

Aside from these rules, keep it relaxed and cool and everyone will be good.

In joining the chat, you acknowledge you understand the rules and that we will enforce these rules.
